  • Automatic Conveyor Belt for Sorting: As an intern at Mahroy, I helped develop an automatic system to sort garment. For this 2-month project, I implemented the PID algorithm to control the conveyor belt by developing C/C++ code in the Arduino Framework, using the ESP32Servo, PID_v1, and PMW libraries.
  • MOSPRO LIV: MOSPRO LIV is a prototype of a car driven by a phone application. This was my capstone project to graduate as an industrial electronics mid-level technician. The car prototype consisted of a small car made by recyclable materials, an Arduino Uno as control unit, an HC-05 Bluetooth module, and two 5-volt DC motors. For direction control, we developed our own H-bridges with 2n3904 transistors and 1N4007 diodes.
